About DRIFT-X

DRIFT-X was born from the shared dream of four friends from the CSE Department at Daffodil International University. What began as casual conversations about our fascination with cars and interest in business quickly turned into a passionate project. We wanted to create something that combines our love for automotive culture with creativity, precision, and entrepreneurship.

Meet the Driving Force

Our team is made up of:

  • Shahriar Rahman – Designer and creative mind behind the visual identity of DRIFT-X.
  • Safin Khan – Designer, working alongside Shahriar to craft stylish and high-quality frames.
  • Seam Talukder – Product and delivery manager, ensuring every product reaches our customers perfectly.
  • Ashiqur Rahman – Market manager, connecting DRIFT-X with car enthusiasts and growing our reach.

Together, we are the driving force behind all aspects of DRIFT-X. From conceptualizing designs, managing production, handling deliveries, to marketing and business operations, we work as a team to make sure every part of the business runs smoothly.

Our Mission and Future

At DRIFT-X, our mission is to bring excitement and passion to car enthusiasts everywhere. We specialize in premium car poster frames, Hot Wheels displays, and fully customized collections. Every product reflects our dedication, creativity, and attention to detail.

Our journey is just beginning. As young entrepreneurs, we are constantly learning, experimenting, and pushing the boundaries of what DRIFT-X can become. We aim to build a brand that celebrates cars, creativity, and community while delivering products that fans proudly display in their homes, offices, or collections.

DRIFT-X is more than a business—it’s a passion, a hobby, and a lifestyle. We live and breathe every aspect of it, and we invite you to join us as we share the thrill of cars with enthusiasts around the world.
